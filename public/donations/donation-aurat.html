Pad Distribution

Break the stigma around menstruation by distributing high-quality sanitary pads to women and girls in underserved communities. Each pack ensures menstrual hygiene, reduces health risks, and enables girls to attend school without interruption. Combined with awareness sessions on menstrual health management.
